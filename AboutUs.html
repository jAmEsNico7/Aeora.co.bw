About Us

Aeora is a multidisciplinary Motswana-owned business that was established in 2015.

We offer a range of internationally renowned brands known for their quality and innovation as part of our global solutions. Our portfolio offers precise and adaptable cutting-edge technologies for narcotics, smart mobility, forensics, law enforcement, and intelligence solutions.
Aeora is dedicated to providing top-notch and dependable goods in all areas of our
solutions portfolio while also aggressively tackling current and upcoming difficulties.

Our company was founded with the goal of building up local expertise in key areas of solution installation, management, and upkeep in the fields of enforcement, traffic management, and intelligent systems.
We take great pride in our staff of talented and highly motivated staff who share a common drive and desire for sheer excellence.
